Our Story

Baking with passion and purpose since 2020

From Humble Beginnings

Sweet Crust Bakery started as a small home kitchen where our founder, Sarah Johnson, baked artisan bread for local farmers markets. What began as a weekend passion project quickly grew as word spread about our quality and attention to detail.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of simple, quality ingredients. Every product we create starts with locally sourced flour, dairy from regional farms, and seasonal fruits. We maintain traditional baking methods while incorporating modern techniques to ensure consistency and excellence.

Community Commitment

As a local business, we're committed to supporting our community. We partner with local suppliers, participate in community events, and donate unsold goods to local shelters at the end of each day.
